About

I'm a product designer who bridges the gap between human needs and intelligent systems. My work combines deep user research, data-driven insights, and rapid prototyping to create experiences that feel both intuitive and transformative. I'm particularly excited about how AI can augment human creativity rather than replace it.

Key Strengths

  • User Research & Synthesis: Conducting interviews, usability testing, and translating insights into actionable design decisions
  • AI/ML Integration: Designing interfaces for LLM-powered tools, understanding prompt engineering and model limitations
  • Data Visualization: Creating clear, compelling dashboards that surface insights for diverse stakeholders
  • Rapid Prototyping: Building high-fidelity prototypes in Figma and functional demos in React/JavaScript
  • Cross-functional Collaboration: Working effectively with engineers, PMs, researchers, and executives

Working Style

  • I start with "why" before jumping to "how" - understanding the problem deeply before proposing solutions
  • I iterate in public - sharing early, rough work to get feedback faster and build collective ownership
  • I balance craft and speed - knowing when to polish details and when to move fast and learn
